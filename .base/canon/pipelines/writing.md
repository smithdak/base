---
id: writing
description: Outline, approve, draft, and deliver a written artifact.
stages:
  - use: intake
  - use: outline
    gate: plan-approval
  - use: draft
  - use: record
---

Use this pipeline when the deliverable is prose for humans — a document, guide, note, or
announcement — rather than code. The outline is the plan: it carries the approval gate, and the
draft must not silently outgrow it.
